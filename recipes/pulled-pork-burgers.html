Pulled Pork Burgers

The perfect weekend lunch for a crowd,
this super tasty and low effort meal is one of our family favourites!

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 4hrs 30 mins | Total time: 4hrs 45 mins
Ingredients
  • 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 2 tbsp Cumin
  • 1.8kg boneless Pork shoulder (we like to cook with rind on)
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp sultans (can use more brown sugar)
  • 3 Garlic cloves, chopped
  • 3 x 410g cans crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 x chillies in adobo sauce (optional)
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 8 x slices of tasty or burger cheese
  • 8 soft bread rolls
  • Optional - coriander and coleslaw to serve
Method
  1. Combine sugar, smoked paprika, cumin and 1 tbs salt in a bowl, then rub over pork shoulder.
    Transfer pork to a large bowl, cover and chill overnight.
  2. The next day, preheat oven to 160Â°C. Heat oil in a heavy-based cast-iron pan over medium heat.
    Add onion and sultanas and cook, stirring, for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  3. Add garlic and cook for a further 30 seconds or until fragrant. Add tomato, chipotle, Worcestershire sauce and vinegar.
    Bring to a simmer, then add the pork shoulder, making sure part of the shoulder sits above sauce.
  4. Place in the oven and roast for 4 hours to 4 hours and 30 minutes or until meat is tender enough to pull apart
    Transfer pork to a tray, cover with foil and rest for 30 minutes
  5. Meanwhile, to make barbecue sauce, use a stick blender to blend braising liquid until smooth.
  6. Using forks, pull apart pork shoulder. Serve on rolls with barbecue sauce, coriander, cheese and coleslaw, if using.
