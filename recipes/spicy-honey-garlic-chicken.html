Spicy Honey Garlic Chicken

EXTRA CRISPY chicken coated in a spicy, sweet,
savoury garlic sauce, this is a forever classic dish!

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 20 mins | Total time: 30 mins

If you never tried this Spicy Honey Garlic Chicken recipe, you are missing out

Ingredients

The Chicken
  • 6 chicken thigh fillets (cut into bite-size pieces)
  • 1/3 cup of Rice flour
  • 1/4 cup of Corn starch
  • 1 tbsp of Garlic powder
  • Salt and Pepper to season
  • 1/3 cup of Soda Water
  • 1 Egg
Homemade sauce
  • 8 cloves of Garlic, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Gochujang
  • 3 tbsp of Honey
  • 1 tbsp of Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp of Sesame Seeds
Steps
  1. Marinate the chicken with salt and pepper, garlic powder, corn starch, rice flour, egg, soda water.
    set aside for 10 minutes
  2. Fry in medium high heat for 4-5 minutes or until golden brown.
    Optional but highly recommended, double fry for another 1-2 minutes after removing from heat for extra crispiness
  3. in a pan, add in Gochujang, Honey, 1 tbsp of soy sauce and minced garlic.
  4. Turn the heat up to a medium-low and mix until everything is thoroughly mixed.
  5. Add in the chicken and make sure every piece of chicken is covered in the sauce.
  6. Finish off with sesame seeds. Saute on high heat for 1 minute.
  7. Add to a plate with steamed medium grain white rice and optional broccolini and serve.
