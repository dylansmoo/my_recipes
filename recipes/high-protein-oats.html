High Protein Overnight Oats

The beauty of overnight oats (and the reason why I love them)
is their simplicity, adaptability and deliciousness

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 0 mins | Total time: 10 mins
Ingredients
  • 2 x scoops (60g) Whey Protein Isolate Vanilla or similar flavour
  • 1 cup quick oats
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 spoon smooth peanut butter
  • Add in dark chocolate, granola or shredded almonds on top
Method
  1. Place Whey Protein Isolate, quick oats, milk and cinnamon in a container and stir well.
  2. Put lid on container and place in the fridge to set overnight.
  3. Swirl peanut butter around the edges of two jars by placing slightly melted peanut butter on a knife
    and rotating around the edges of the jar.
  4. Spoon in overnight oats and sprinkle with your choice of toppings.
